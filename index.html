    Frequently Asked Questions

    Questions? We've Got
    Answers.

    Everything you need to know before getting started.

    How much does it cost?

    +

    Nothing upfront — ever. We take a small percentage of the new revenue our agents generate for your store. If we don't grow your revenue, you owe us nothing. The risk is entirely on us.

    How do you connect to my Shopify store?

    +

    We integrate directly via Shopify's API — no plugins to install, no code to touch. We also connect to Klaviyo, Postscript, and your existing email/SMS stack so everything works inside your current setup.

    How fast will I see results?

    +

    Abandoned cart and win-back flows typically generate revenue within the first week of going live. Full results from all four agents compound over 60–90 days. We move fast because we don't get paid until you do.

    I'm already running Klaviyo flows. Will this conflict?

    +

    No — we audit what you already have, plug the gaps, and enhance your existing flows rather than replace them. Most brands we work with have basic flows running but are missing the personalization and timing that actually converts.

    How do you track what revenue you generated?

    +

    You get a live dashboard showing every dollar our agents recover and generate — with full attribution. Revenue from abandoned cart recovery, win-back campaigns, upsells, and new customer acquisition is tracked separately so you always know exactly what you're paying for.

    Do you work with Amazon FBA sellers?

    +

    Yes — we specialize in helping FBA sellers build off-Amazon revenue through email list building, DTC storefronts, and content that drives external traffic. Reducing your Amazon dependency is one of the highest-ROI moves a seller can make.

    Is there a contract?

    +

    We ask for a 3-month commitment so our agents have time to build, learn, and optimize. After that it's month-to-month — cancel anytime, no questions asked.

    Will my customers know it's AI?

    +

    No — every message is written in your brand voice, your tone, and your style. We spend time during onboarding learning how your brand communicates, because one robotic message can cost you a customer for life.

    How is this different from a marketing agency?

    +

    Agencies charge $3K–$10K/month whether they deliver or not — and most focus on driving new traffic while ignoring the revenue you're already leaking. We focus on monetizing what you already have, and we only get paid when we succeed.

    What if it doesn't work?

    +

    Then you don't pay. Our entire fee is a percentage of new revenue we generate — if the agents don't produce, we earn nothing. We've structured it this way on purpose: the only way we win is if you win first.
